The release completed, but the final record only says "looks good." Replace that with evidence from the verification window and a final production state.

Your task:

1. **Record the verification window** for the post-release check.
2. **Add observed metrics** for 5xx rate, checkout success, p95 latency, payment provider errors, and new error patterns.
3. **Record the final state** with task definition, traffic, verifier, and timestamp.

The grader checks the verification snapshot and final state.
