Before creating Azure resources, confirm which subscription and tenant the CLI is using. The expected subscription is `sub-devpolaris-training`, and the tenant is `tenant-devpolaris`.

Your job:

1. **Inspect the active Azure account** from the CLI.
2. **Confirm the subscription ID** is `sub-devpolaris-training`.
3. **Confirm the tenant ID** is `tenant-devpolaris`.

The grader checks Azure account evidence from the CLI output.
