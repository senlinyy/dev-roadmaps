title: "Classify TLS Handshake and Trust Failures"
sectionSlug: when-http-and-tls-break

---

TLS incidents look similar until you separate trust-store failures, hostname mismatches, missing intermediates, SNI mistakes, protocol mismatches, mixed content, and clock skew. Read the evidence the way you would during a production escalation. Choose the failure mode that best explains the observed client output.
