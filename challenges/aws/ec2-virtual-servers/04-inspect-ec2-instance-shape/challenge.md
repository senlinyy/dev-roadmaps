The `devpolaris-orders-api` instance already exists as `i-orders-api-01`. Your first job is not to fix it, but to read the shape AWS says it has.

Your job:

1. **Describe the instance** and check its type, subnet, security group, and private address.
2. **Describe the instance status** so you can separate "created" from "healthy enough to inspect."

The grader checks that you gathered the server-shaped evidence from AWS.
