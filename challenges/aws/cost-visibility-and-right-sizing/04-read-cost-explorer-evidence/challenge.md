The finance review asks why orders spend moved in May 2026. Use Cost Explorer for the period `2026-05-01` to `2026-06-01`, monthly granularity, and service-tag grouping.

Your job:

1. **Inspect cost usage** for the May 2026 period.
2. **Group the evidence** by service ownership so the orders service can be separated from shared platform spend.
3. **Keep the output visible** so the grader can see the cost groups and totals.

The grader checks AWS cost evidence, not a written explanation.
