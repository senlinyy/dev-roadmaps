Define clear role defaults for the orders web role contract.

Requirements:

1. **Service:** `orders_service_name: devpolaris-orders-api`.
2. **Port:** `orders_api_port: 8080`.
3. **Server name:** `orders_server_name: orders.devpolaris.internal`.
